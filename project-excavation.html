Rover Excavation & Construction Payload

Designed and built the excavation and construction payload for the UTS Rover, responsible for digging and depositing regolith for the Exterres Crater Lab challenge at the Australian Rover Challenge. The payload contributed to winning the Exterres Crater Lab Challenge and qualifying for the NASA Lunabotics competition.
